# Abdominal Pain

Pain that occurs between the chest and pelvic regions. Abdominal pain can be cramp-like, achy, dull, intermittent or sharp. Also called a stomachache.

## Common Causes (Non-Emergency)

- Indigestion
- Constipation
- Stomach virus
- Menstrual cramps
- Gas
- Food allergies

## When to Seek Help

Seek immediate medical help if:

- Pain is severe and sudden.
- Pain is accompanied by bloody stools, persistent nausea and vomiting, yellow skin/eyes, swelling of the abdomen, or difficulty breathing.
- You are pregnant.
- The abdomen is tender to the touch.
- The pain is the result of an injury to the abdomen.
